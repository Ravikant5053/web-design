Author Biodata - Mark Twain

Full Name: Samuel Langhorne Clemens

Pen Name: Mark Twain

Date of Birth: November 30, 1835

Date of Death: April 21, 1910

Nationality: American

Notable Works: Adventures of Huckleberry Finn, The Adventures of Tom Sawyer, The Prince and the Pauper, etc.

Biography: Mark Twain was an American author and humorist. He is best known for his novels The Adventures of Tom Sawyer and Adventures of Huckleberry Finn, which are often called the Great American Novels.
